Course Description

If you have vision to work with foreign cars and dealers, we have tailored the perfect altered programs for you. We offer innovative and realistic training in car scanning diagnosis, vehicle sensors and vehicle networking systems etc. Modern vehicles have computer processors, chips and sensors so if you want to inspect such vehicles and want to know what is really going on, a diagnostic scan with a scan tool. reveals the problems associated with all electronic and related parts of the vehicle. This guidance will enable you to handle electronically controlled vehicles confidently

What you'll learn

  • Skills to become an Automobile Technician
  • Automobile Fundamentals
  • Emission standards(BS3.4.6)
  • Module diagnosing
  • Vehicle network system
  • Automotive sensors
